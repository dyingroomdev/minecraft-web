🛡 Community Charter

Server Rules & Fair Play

Friendly vibes keep AmzCraft thriving. Know the rules, play with honor, and keep the realm a place worth logging into.

📌 Pinned Rules
Notify Officials Before Declaring War
General
📌 Pinned
Before initiating any guild war or territory conflict, you must inform Server Officials in the designated Discord channel at least 30 minutes in advance. Unannounced attacks may be rolled back without warning.
Rule #001
No Griefing in Protected Zones
General
📌 Pinned
Destroying, stealing from, or altering builds in spawn, market, and event zones is strictly prohibited regardless of your rank. Violations result in a permanent ban with no appeal.
Rule #002
Respect All Players Equally
Conduct
📌 Pinned
Harassment, discrimination, or targeted toxicity toward any player will result in an immediate temporary ban. Repeat offenders receive a permanent ban. This applies in-game, Discord, and all AmzCraft spaces.
Rule #003
⚔ Combat & PvP
Combat Logging is Forbidden
PvP
Logging out during active combat to avoid death is considered combat logging. Automatic systems will kill your character anyway and log the offense.
Rule #101
No Kill-on-Sight in Safe Zones
PvP
The spawn market radius (150 blocks) and event arenas during non-PvP events are no-kill zones. Staff will reverse kills and penalize offenders.
Rule #102
War Notifications are Mandatory
Guilds
Guild leaders must post a war declaration in #war-declarations on Discord before any coordinated attack. Include target guild name, estimated start time, and reason.
Rule #103
No Teaming in Solo PvP Events
Events
Solo tournament brackets prohibit coordinated attacks with other players. Working with allies in a solo event is grounds for immediate disqualification.
Rule #104
🔧 Cheating & Exploits
Zero Tolerance for Hacked Clients
Cheating
Using any client with fly hacks, speed hacks, kill aura, auto-clicker, or X-ray is a permanent ban with no appeal. We deploy continuous anti-cheat monitoring.
Rule #201
Report Exploits, Don't Use Them
Exploits
If you discover a duplication glitch or server exploit, report it to staff immediately. Exploiting for personal gain is a temporary-to-permanent ban depending on severity.
